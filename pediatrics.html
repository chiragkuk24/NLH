Pediatrics Department

Specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents in a child‑friendly environment.

Comprehensive Pediatric Care

Our Pediatrics Department is dedicated to providing exceptional medical care for children from birth through adolescence. We understand that children have unique medical needs and require specialized attention.

Our team of board‑certified pediatricians and child specialists are trained to handle a wide range of childhood illnesses, developmental issues, and preventive care.

  • Newborn and infant care
  • Childhood vaccinations and immunizations
  • Growth and development monitoring
  • Management of childhood diseases
  • Adolescent health services
  • Emergency pediatric care

Our Pediatric Services

We offer a comprehensive range of pediatric services to ensure your child's health and well‑being.

Neonatal Care

Specialized care for newborns, including premature baby care, neonatal intensive care, and newborn screening.

Well‑Child Visits

Regular check‑ups to monitor growth, development, and provide age‑appropriate vaccinations.

Pediatric Cardiology

Diagnosis and treatment of heart conditions in children, including congenital heart defects.

Developmental Pediatrics

Assessment and management of developmental delays, learning disabilities, and behavioral issues.
